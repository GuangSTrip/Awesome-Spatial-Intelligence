## Introduction

Spatial Intelligence is becoming increasingly important in the field of Artificial Intelligence. This repository aims to provide a comprehensive and systematic collection of research related to Spatial Intelligence. We hope that this work can provide a quick way and guide for those who want to get up to speed with the field of spatial intelligence.

We now offer our V2 repository architecture, providing relevant resources at every area in spatial intelligence to ensure researchers can easily access the knowledge they need.

The repository is continuously being developed and expanded. Any suggestion is welcome, please feel free to raise an issue. ^_^
